What Is LifeLock Account Recovery?
==================================

LifeLock account recovery is the process of restoring access when you cannot sign in to your account. The recovery process helps verify your identity, update your login information, and allow you to securely access your account again.

Account recovery may be required when:

* You forget your password.
* Your login credentials no longer work.
* Your account is locked.
* You suspect unauthorized access.
* You cannot complete sign in verification.
* Your security information needs updating.

LifeLock Account Recovery: 7 Steps to Get Back Into Your Account
================================================================

1. Check Your Login Information
------------------------------

Start by verifying that you are entering the correct email address and password.

Check for:

* Incorrect spelling in your email address.
* Accidental spaces.
* Password typing errors.
* Incorrect account credentials.

Correct login details may immediately resolve access issues.

2. Reset Your Forgotten Password
--------------------------------

If you cannot remember your password, use the password recovery option to create a new one.

When creating a new password:

* Choose a unique password.
* Avoid using previous passwords.
* Use a strong combination of characters.
* Store your password securely.

A password reset is one of the most common recovery methods.

3. Complete Identity Verification
---------------------------------

LifeLock may require identity verification before restoring account access.

Follow the verification instructions carefully and confirm your account ownership.

Make sure your recovery information is accurate, including:

* Registered email address.
* Phone number.
* Other account verification details.

4. Fix Two Factor Authentication Issues
---------------------------------------

If you are unable to complete two factor authentication, check your verification method.

Try the following:

* Request a new verification code.
* Confirm your phone number or email address.
* Check your internet or mobile connection.
* Use the latest verification code received.

Avoid entering expired or previously used codes.

5. Clear Browser Problems
-------------------------

Browser issues can sometimes prevent successful account recovery.

Try these solutions:

* Clear browser cache and cookies.
* Update your browser.
* Disable unnecessary extensions.
* Try another supported browser.

A browser problem may affect login pages or verification steps.

6. Recover Access From a Trusted Device
---------------------------------------

Using a familiar device can help complete the recovery process.

Try signing in from:

* A previously used computer.
* A trusted smartphone.
* A familiar browser.
* A secure network connection.

Trusted devices may reduce verification problems.

7. Request Additional Account Assistance
----------------------------------------

If you cannot recover your account using standard methods, additional verification may be required.

Prepare the following information:

* Registered email address.
* Account details.
* Description of the problem.
* Error messages.
* Recent account activity.

Additional assistance can help verify ownership and restore access.

Common LifeLock Account Recovery Problems
==========================================

---------------------------------

Recovery emails may be delayed or filtered into spam or junk folders.

Solution:

* Check all email folders.
* Confirm your email address.
* Request another password reset message.

Incorrect Recovery Information
------------------------------

Outdated contact details may prevent identity verification.

Solution:

Update recovery information after regaining access.

Verification Code Problems
---------------------------

Expired or incorrect codes may prevent successful verification.

Solution:

Request a new code and enter it promptly.

Account Locked During Recovery
------------------------------

Security restrictions may temporarily prevent access.

Solution:

Wait before trying again and complete required verification steps.

Browser Compatibility Issues
-----------------------------

Old browser settings may interfere with account recovery.

Solution:

Update your browser or try another device.
